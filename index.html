Everything you need to know about TaxGo

LHDN (Lembaga Hasil Dalam Negeri) e-invoice is Malaysia's mandatory electronic invoicing system for tax compliance. TaxGo simplifies the entire process by automating submission, ensuring compliance, and providing multiple integration options so you can focus on your business instead of administrative tasks.
